Sex Ed Inc. - Inclusive Sex and Relationships Education


We’re a not-for-profit organisation that is dedicated to bringing open, accurate, and inclusive information about sex and relationships to teens across the UK. We believe that everyone deserves the possibility to participate equally in safe, healthy, fulfilling relationships and that being well-informed is a central part of having this opportunity. Sex Education that promotes incomplete, stereotypical or insensitive information harms young people and has no place in our society or our schools.


“Our Sex and Relationships classes had all been about how not to avoid getting pregnant, nobody ever talked about what it would be like to have sex if you were two women, and not a woman and a man. So having the facilitators bring that up in the workshop made me feel like I could actually ask questions that were relevant to me or that I was worrying about.” - Susannah, 14
